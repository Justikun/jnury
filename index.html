WHAT DOES JNURY MEAN?

The name JNURY serves as a reminder of God's glory in our pursuit of both personal and spiritual journeys.

J

The J in JNURY signifies Justin, a personal touch, and Jasper, a gemstone deeply revered for its spiritual significance.

In Revelation 21, the Apostle John is shown New Jerusalem—a holy city that will descend from heaven at the start of the Millennium. He speaks of the city having God's glory, comparing it to a jasper stone—clear as crystal. He continues by describing the wall of the city, which has twelve foundations, each representing one of Christ’s twelve apostles. The first foundation of the wall is Jasper.

NURY

Derived from the Hebrew word Nuri, meaning my fire, it powerfully reflects the spiritual symbol of God’s glory and His beckon to holiness as depicted with Moses and the burning bush.

My fire also embodies the drive that fuels the pursuit of one’s goals, igniting purpose in every endeavor.
